My Insta Ramen Recipe

This is my recipe on making instant ramen

Ingredients

Grocery List within a div:

I'm using classes!

  • Choice of bowl
  • Your choice of instant ramen
    • Chicken
    • Beef
    • Ect..
  • Water to fill your dish
  • Oven mitts
  • Microwave

Now for the cooking process

1. Open ramen package and seperate the ramen and flavoring
2. Set flavoring to the side and put ramen into the bowl in a manner were it won't break in half
3. Fill up bowl with water until it reaches a little before the top
4. Microwave for 3 minutes on high
5. Carefully take out the bowl with oven mitts and empty as much water as you can
6. Once the water is drained put your flavoring onto the ramen and stir until it looks fully mixed
7. Wait until it cools off and enjoy!!
